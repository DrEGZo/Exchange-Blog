Privacy policy

Definitions

This privacy policy applies to all pages and subpages of the Exchange Blog website ("website" / "site").

All visitors of the site are considered "users", especially those who have access to the protected area.

Any possibility of contact ("by mail") refers to the email adress given in the site note.

The "administrator" is the responsible contact person mentioned in the site note.

Google Cloud Firestore

When registering on the website, all users have to provide some personal data and get the chance to subscribe to email notifications / newsletters. Whith exception of email and password, all of these data are stored in the Google Cloud Firestore and are maintained by the administrator.​

The real name is used to identify a user by the administrator and as a salutation in newsletters. It will not be visible to any other users.

The username (nickname) is used for writing comments on the website. Therefore a users's username will be displayed next to comments written by this user.

With exception of the real name, all of these data can be altered afterwards in the profile settings. This implies the possibility to opt out from the newsletters.

Google Firebase services

This site works using Google Firebase services. These services are necessary to provide static files, an application programming interface, an authentication system, a database and usage statistics. The used services are listed beneath:

Google Cloud Functions for Firebase, Firebase Authentication, Firebase Hosting, Google Analytics for Firebase

More information about the usage and the storage of personal data by these services can be found here: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy/#pii-table

Data and third-party services

This website is using several third-party services such as Imgur (https://imgur.com), YouTube (https://www.youtube.com) and further Google services. Those services are used to embed videos and images, for example. The reason of this use is that media needing much memory can be swaped to third-party clouds in order to relieve this website (as for memory) and to improve the functionality.

The use of those third-party services means also that those parties are using cookies and usage data for their services.

Cookies

In order to ensure the functionality, this wesite uses cookies. Cookies are small text files being stored by your browser to reauthenticate a user on his next site visit. They are used for authentication but also for third-party services. If you do not agree with the use of cookies, you can delete all cookies set by this site from your browser memory and do not visit this website again.

Change, deletion and disclosure

Some data provided at the registration can be changed manually in the profile settings. This implies the possibility to opt out from newsletters. Any further personal data can be changed on request by email, as long as they are managed by the administrator.

You may also request an account deletion by email. All data being stored in the Google Cloud Firestore as well as your email and password will be deleted from the system. Please mind that this cannot be undone.​

Any further data not being managed by the administrator cannot be deleted immediately as they are controled by third parties. Please contact the appropriate service provider.​

A disclosure of all stored data being managed by the administrator can be requested by email. The information will be given within 30 days according to the GDPR.

Revocation of the privacy policy

As long as you are using your account or visiting the website, you agree to this private policy. If you want to revoke your agreement, please follow the steps of an account deletion. After your account has been deleted, you are not bound any longer to this privacy policy, assuming you also stop any use of this site.

Obscurities

If there are any ambiguities or you still have questions, please contact us by email.
